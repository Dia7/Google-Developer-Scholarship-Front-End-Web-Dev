Chameleon

Chameleons feed by ballistically projecting their tongues often over twice the length of their body to catch prey.

  • Scientific name: Chamaeleonidae
  • Average length: 35 Centimeters
  • Average lifespan: 5 years
  • Habitat: Range from rain forest to desert

Different chameleon species are able to vary their coloration and pattern through combinations of pink, blue, red, orange, green, black, brown, light blue, yellow, turquoise, and purple.
